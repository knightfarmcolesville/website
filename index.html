Welcome to the Colesville Restoration Site

This is the Joseph Knight Sr and Josiah Stowell Colesville Branch Homes

Welcome!

We're so happy to came to our website. Whether you've been to the Knight Farm before or it's your first time, you'll find everything you need to plan your visit and/or support our efforts to make this remarkable historical site available to all.

Plan Your Visit

We had an outstanding 2025 season, exceeding all expectations with visitors. The homes are closed for the winter, but we reopen in May. We hope to have many tour groups and families stop by to see the homes and feel the sweet spirit of these historic homes. Also, please contact us if you are interested in a custom off-season tour!

Thank you to our Docents who give their time to come and serve, they do a great job telling the story of the faith and sacrifice of the Knight family. For information on becoming a docent – follow this link: Docent Information Page

For questions or more information email colesvillerestoration@gmail.com
