Welcome to the Colesville Restoration Site

This is the Joseph Knight Sr and Josiah Stowell Colesville Branch Homes

Plan Your Visit

We had an outstanding 2025 season, we exceeded all expectations with how many visitors we had. We have the homes closed for the winter, but we expect next summer to be another busy year. We hope to have many tour groups and families stop by to see the homes and feel the sweet spirit of these historic homes.

Thank you to our Docents who give their time to come and serve, they do a great job telling the story of the faith and sacrifice of the Knight family.
